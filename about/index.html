About Us

Founded in Fall 2011, Purdue Special Interest Group Game Development is a multi-disciplinary collaborative group of Purdue University students that creates games as our own development studio. As part of the Association for Computing Machinery Purdue chapter, we are the first SIGGD of ACM National. Our goal is to develop creative and interesting games.
